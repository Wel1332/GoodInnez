### 1. **Hotel Routes**

* `GET /api/hotels` – Get all hotels
* `GET /api/hotels/{id}` – Get a hotel by ID
* `POST /api/hotels` – Create a new hotel
* `PUT /api/hotels/{id}` – Update a hotel by ID
* `DELETE /api/hotels/{id}` – Delete a hotel by ID
